# Phase 3 — Sales and Collections

## Objective

Implement Omni Mini-ERP's operational sales workflow from quotation through collection, receivable monitoring, government deductions, and source-document traceability.

## Dependencies

- Phase 0 completed
- Phase 1 completed and validated
- Phase 2 completed and validated

## Work Packages

1. WP-03-01 — Sales Settings and Workflow
2. WP-03-02 — Quotations
3. WP-03-03 — Sales Orders
4. WP-03-04 — Delivery Records
5. WP-03-05 — Sales Invoices
6. WP-03-06 — Customer Payments and Allocations
7. WP-03-07 — Receivables and Aging
8. WP-03-08 — Government Sales Deductions and Withholding
9. WP-03-09 — Sales Attachments and Traceability
10. WP-03-10 — Phase Validation and Gap Review

## Phase Boundaries

This phase may create operational receivable balances but must not implement:

- Inventory movements or costing
- Cost of sales
- General-ledger posting
- Financial statements
- Percentage-tax return calculations
- BIR electronic filing
- Purchasing or supplier payments

## Definition of Done

All work packages pass fresh migrations, tests, Pint, PHPStan, frontend build, authorization review, and Phase 3 validation.
